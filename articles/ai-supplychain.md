## What Is Artificial Intelligence in Supply Chain?

Artificial intelligence in supply chain refers to the application of AI technologies—including machine learning, predictive analytics, and advanced data modeling—to optimize supply chain processes from procurement to delivery.

### Role of AI, ML, and Predictive Analytics

- **Machine learning in supply chain**: Identifies patterns in historical and real-time data to improve forecasting accuracy  
- **Predictive analytics in supply chain**: Anticipates demand fluctuations, supplier risks, and logistical challenges  
- **AI-powered supply chain systems**: Automate repetitive tasks and enhance decision-making with data-driven insights  

These technologies enable businesses to create intelligent, agile, and responsive supply chain operations.

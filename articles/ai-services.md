## Role of an AI Engineer & AI Developer

Successful AI projects rely on skilled professionals. Understanding the roles involved helps businesses plan better.

### AI Engineer Responsibilities

An AI engineer focuses on designing and deploying AI systems at scale.

**Key skills:**
- Machine learning algorithms  
- Data pipelines and MLOps  
- Cloud AI infrastructure  

### AI Developer Responsibilities

An AI developer implements AI models into applications and products.

**Key skills:**
- Programming (Python, Java, R)  
- API integration  
- Model implementation  

### AI Engineer vs. AI Developer

| AI Engineer | AI Developer |
|------------|--------------|
| Focuses on system architecture | Focuses on application development |
| Handles scalability & deployment | Integrates AI into products |
| Works on infrastructure & MLOps | Works on features & functionality |

Both roles are essential for delivering robust AI development services.

### How long does AI development take?

AI development timelines vary. A basic AI prototype may take 6–8 weeks, while full-scale custom AI solutions can take several months.
